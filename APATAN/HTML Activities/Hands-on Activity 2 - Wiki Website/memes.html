Why does it matter?

It sounds a bit ironic to ask why a nihilist meme matters, but there’s a reason these birds are everywhere.

Most memes are either loud and angry or just random. The Nihilist Penguin is different it’s gentle. It gives people a way to talk about feeling overwhelmed by the world without being "doom-and-gloom" about it.

By laughing at how small we are compared to the universe, it actually makes our daily stresses (like that 11:59 PM deadline) feel a little more manageable. It's not about giving up; it's about realizing that if nothing matters, we’re finally free to just be ourselves.
